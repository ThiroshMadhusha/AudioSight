Welcome to The Audio Sight

Mobile Base Sinhala Book Reader For Visually Impaired Individuals



Why Choose The AudioSight ?

You selected the AudioSight App for our Sinhala Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Sinhala Text to Speech Synthesizer (TTS), Sinhala Voice Navigation, and Object Detection, as well as Sinhala Image Identification and Captioning for our Blind Story Book Reader mobile application due to its exceptional capabilities in enhancing accessibility and usability for the visually impaired community.

Sinhala Optical Character Recognition (OCR)

A blind user takes a photo of a Sinhala text document using their smartphone. The AudioSight App swiftly recognizes the text and converts it into audible content, allowing the user to access and understand the information within seconds.

Sinhala Text to Speech Synthesizer (TTS)

A visually impaired individual receives a Sinhala e-book. They employ the AudioSight App to convert the written text into spoken words, enabling them to enjoy literature and access educational content seamlessly.

Sinhala Voice Navigation & Object Detection

A blind user aims to navigate an unfamiliar environment. The AudioSight App assists by providing clear Sinhala voice navigation instructions, guiding them through streets, public transport, and indoor spaces with confidence.

Sinhala Image Identification & Captioning

A visually impaired person uses the app's object detection and Sinhala image identification features describe these items, allowing users to maintain their independence and manage their surroundings effectively.

About the Project

Read our research domain details here

Enhancing Accessibility for the visually impaired by introducing a new app called "AudioSight" to the Community.

Our project focuses on revolutionizing accessibility for the visually impaired community through the utilization of the AudioSight App. We have chosen this application for its remarkable capabilities in Sinhala Optical Character Recognition (OCR), Sinhala Text to Speech Synthesis (TTS), Sinhala Voice Navigation, and Sinhala Object Detection and Image Identification.

In the Sinhala OCR scenario, the app swiftly converts captured text into audible content, allowing users to access written information effortlessly. The Sinhala TTS feature empowers visually impaired individuals to explore story books and educational materials. The Sinhala Voice Navigation function provides essential guidance in unfamiliar environments, while the Sinhala Object Detection and Image Identification features make everyday tasks more manageable. This project is a step towards making the world more inclusive, granting the visually impaired community equal access to information and a newfound sense of independence.

Literature Survey

In dairy farming industry, cattle play an important role. According to the Livestock bulletin_2017 there are total 332,335 cattle in Sri Lanka. The milk production in Sri Lanka during 2017 was 404,600,001.

Cattle are considered as the most useful domestic animals since the ancient times. Ancient humans as well as in the present world we get so many behefits from cattle. After human mother's milk, cow milk is the source of having so many nutrients which helps in,

Helps in making teeth and bones strong

Helps in making teeth and bones strong

Helps in keeping good heart health

Boosts up the immune system

Prevent from diabetes

Helps in the development of children's brain.

Therefore having no cattle on this earth is like losing a key source of nutrients and lose of many other benefits. Having no cattle in the world affects on environmental damage as well as the economy.

Cattle diseases are one of the greatest problems in dairy farms. Due to different types of reasons such as bacteria, parasites, fungi, chemical poisons and etc. diseases are spreading among the cattle in Sri Lanka as well as in other countries. As a result of this number of cattle die and it causes economic, environmental and social damage. Also, cattle diseases can affect human health. Mostly when individual farmers own cattle herds, there is a high probability to have cattle deaths due to diseases.

This situation can be controlled if farmers have ability to identify the exact disease of cattle. And by having the solution for the disease, farmers can give a cure and control the disease to a certain point. According to the findings of the survey study we found out that there is an unavailability of a proper mobile application to analyze and identify the exact type of the disease of cattle and give solutions

Research Gap

There are several researches which are conducted about cattle farm management. Most of the time most of them have mainly focused on production. Among the researches done so far, the following areas were focused.

Records of the milk production

Dairy products

Income and expenses of dairy products

1. EMA-I: A mobile application for timely animal disease field reporting to enhance surveillance. This study holds smart phones to collect animal disease information and generate reports about the collected data. And afterwards send them to the Global Animal Disease Information System. Here it is more like collecting disease information and store them, but this app does not suggest treatments and prevention methods to overcome the diseases

2. My cattle Manager: This mobile application mainly focuses on keeping records of the farm, milk productivity, income and expenses

3. Cattle expert system: This is also a mobile application which is designed to find cattle diseases. Here there are already added images of diseases which cattle can be diagnosed with. The user must select the symptoms and get the result [16]. But do not allow the users to upload their own cow's diseased areas to the system

Therefore the main gap between our system and the exsising systems are that, there are no proper mobile application to identify breeds and diseases by uploading images and videos of cattle.

Research Problem

Research problem is devided in to two categories -> Breeds Identification & Disease Identification

There is no proper mobile application to identify breeds using images and diseases of a cow using images and videos.

Research Objectives

Main Objective

Implement a mobile application which identify diseases by analyzing images, videos (to detect the behavioral changes) using image processing and machine learning techniques, and recommending treatments for identified diseases. And also identify the cattle breed by analyzing uploaded images of cattle.

Sub Objectives

Analyze images to identify cattle breed by considering characteristics such as color, body size, tail, neck etc. using image processing


Analyze the videos and detect behavioral changes to identify the diseases and provide medical advices & prevention methods.


Analyze images to identify the disease and provide medical advices & prevention methods


Considering factors such as age and weight of diseased cattle which are identified through image processing and prescribe medical advices and medicine.

Methodology

When considering the overall progress of our research project,it consist of four main components. The final product of the research project is a mobile application which provide facilities to identify the dairy cattle breeds and diseases by uploading images and videos.

Identifying cattle breed is important when deciding a disease as there are specific diseases for different kind of breeds when considering their genetics. Therefore this application consist of the component where user can upload a image of a cow and get to know about the exact breed of the cow.

In addition we have implemented the most valuable function of the mobile application where user can upload a image of a cow's diseased area and identify the disease. And user can get to know more details about the identified disease. The other component is identifying a disease using videos of a cow. In this, the behavioral change of a diseased cow is considered to identify the diseases. The user has to upload a video or capture avideo of the cow whoes behavior is bit more different from their normal behavior, and the system is predicting the disease after analyzing the uploaded video.

Further more we have implemented a function where user can identify the age and weight of a cow and predict the best medicine dosage for a identified disease. Users have to upload a image of a cow's mouth(teeth) to identify the age of the cow because a cow's age is decided using their teeth. And to identify the weight user has to upload a image of the cow's body. Then the system analyze the uploaded images and predict the age and weight range and provide the medicine for the particular age and weight range.
